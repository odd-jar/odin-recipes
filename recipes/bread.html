Whole Wheat Bread

A simple, no frills bread recipe adapted from years of baking simple loaves of bread.

This recipe comes from hundreds of loaves of bread I've baked. Its the most simple recipe I use and is definetly not super special, but it will get the job done, especially if you need to multiply it and make many loaves. I usually make this into a round loaf, but occasionaly use a bread pan and make a sandwich-style loaf.

Ingredients*

  • ~ 3⅓ cups (400 grams) of King Arthur Whole Wheat Flour
  • ~ 1½ cups (350mL) of warm water
  • ~ 2 Tbsp (30 mL) of honey
  • ~ 2 Tsp (11 grams) of salt
  • ~ 2¼ tsp (6.75 grams) of yeast

    • *All are approximations because I am liberal with my measurements!
