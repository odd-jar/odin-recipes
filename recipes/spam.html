Hot & Spicy Spam Stir-Fry

Hot and Spicy Spam stir-fry, quick and easy.

An easy, budget-friendly recipe for hot and spicy Spam (the best one). Can be made with fresh rice or, better yet, made with day or two old rice.

Ingredients

  • 1 can of Hot and Spicy Spam
  • 2 cans of mixed vegetables (or fresh veggies of your choice
  • 1/4 stick of butter
  • 1 cup of white rice
  • 2 cups of water
